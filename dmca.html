DMCA Takedown Procedure

We respect intellectual property rights and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you believe that content hosted on this website infringes your copyright, please follow the steps below to submit a takedown request.

1. Identify the Infringing Content

Provide clear information about the copyrighted work you believe has been infringed. Include:

  • Title of the copyrighted work
  • Description or screenshot of the infringing content
  • URL(s) of the infringing material on our website

2. Provide Your Contact Information

Include your full legal name, mailing address, phone number, and email address. This is required so we can contact you regarding your claim.

3. Submit a Written Statement

Your notice must include the following statements under penalty of perjury:

  1. I have a good faith belief that the disputed use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  2. The information in this notice is accurate, and I am the copyright owner or am authorized to act on behalf of the owner.

4. Provide an Electronic or Physical Signature

Include a digital signature (typed full name) or a physical signature if sending a mailed notice.

5. Submit Your DMCA Notice

Send your completed notice to our designated DMCA agent:

Email: dmca@playgrid.com
Mail: 123 Example Street, City, State, ZIP, Country

6. Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification under 17 U.S.C §512(g). Include:

  • Your contact information
  • Identification of the removed material
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake or misidentification
  • Your consent to jurisdiction in your federal district court
  • Your physical or electronic signature

7. Important Notes

  • Do not submit false claims; false notices may lead to legal liability.
  • Processing times may vary, but we aim to respond within 7 business days.
  • All notices and counter-notices are documented and reviewed carefully.
